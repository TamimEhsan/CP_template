## Simple tricks

- gcd(F(n),F(m)) = F(gcd(n,m)) where F(n) is nth fib with first two term as 0,1
- a+b=a⊕b+2(a&b)
- a+b=a|b+a&b
- a⊕b=a|b—a&b
- gcd(a,b) = gcd(a,a-b) = gcd(a,a%b)
